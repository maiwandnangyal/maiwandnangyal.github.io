Maiwand Nangyal

DPhil candidate in Economics at the University of Oxford

Welcome to my website! I'm Maiwand Nangyal, a DPhil candidate in Economics at the University of Oxford, generously funded by the ESRC.

My research explores the intersections of economic history, development economics, political economy, applied econometrics, and machine learning - with a particular focus on the long-run economic impact of colonial institutions in western British India.

I am supervised by Professor Stephen Broadberry and Dr Eric Chaney, and I am proud to be a member of Wolfson College.
