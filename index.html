About

I am a DPhil candidate in Economics at the University of Oxford and am grateful to be funded by the ESRC.

My research focuses on Economic History, Development Economics, Political Economy, Applied Econometrics and Machine Learning. I am particularly interested in investigating the economic impact of colonial institutions in eastern British India.

I am supervised by Professor Stephen Broadberry and Dr Eric Chaney and am a member of Wolfson College.
